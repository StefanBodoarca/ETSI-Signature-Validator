Generate an RSA key for the CA:
$ openssl genrsa -out example.org.key 2048

 Inspect the key:
 $ openssl rsa -in example.org.key -noout -text

 Extract rsa public key can be extracted from the private key:
 $ openssl rsa -in example.org.key -pubout -out example.org.pubkey

 Inspect the public key:
 $ openssl rsa -in example.org.pubkey -pubin -noout -text

 Generate a CSR:
 $ openssl req -new -key example.org.key -out example.org.csr

 Inspect CSR:
 $ openssl req -in example.org.csr -noout -text

 CSR from config file:
 $ openssl req -new -out example.csr -config csr_file.conf

Generate a self signed certificate for the CA:
$ openssl req -new -x509 -key ca.key -out ca.crt

Sign a certificate:
$ openssl x509 -req -in example.org.csr -extfile v3.ext -CA ca.crt -CAkey ca.key -CAcreateserial -out example.org.crt

Examine .crt:
$ openssl x509 -in example.org.crt -noout -text
